# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product summary

Country Analytics Platform helps users explore country indicators and generate evidence-based insights for planning, benchmarking, and decision support.

## Core scope

- Dashboard
- Global Analytics
- Analytics Assistant
- PESTEL and Porter
- Business Analytics

## Primary outcomes

- Faster time-to-insight
- Better trust through grounded outputs
- Report-ready strategic narratives

## Risks and mitigation

- Hallucination risk -> grounding + fallback
- Data lag risk -> data-year transparency
- Misinterpretation risk -> guardrails and clarity
